SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities

Use Cases and Mapping

1. Livable Area Focused on Families and Community Well-Being

Mapped Target: Target 11.7 - Accessible Green and Public Spaces

Reason: Creating spaces for families and communities ensures inclusivity and promotes well-being. Green and public spaces enhance the livability of urban areas.

2. A Hub for Knowledge Sharing and Workforce Training

Mapped Target: Target 11.C - Financial Support for Sustainable Urban Development

Reason: Knowledge-sharing hubs help educate communities and encourage innovation. These initiatives contribute to sustainable urban growth.

3. A Zone for Healthcare and Well-Being

Mapped Target: Target 11.2 - Safe and Sustainable Transportation

Reason: Developing accessible healthcare zones ensures better urban living. Transportation systems should connect people to these zones efficiently.
