Goals and SDG Integration with Indicators

Livable Areas for Families and Community

Relevant SDG: SDG 3: Good Health and Well-Being

Indicator 3.9.1: Reduction in mortality rates from pollution-related diseases.

  • We aim to design neighborhoods that reduce air pollution by including more green spaces like parks and trees.
  • Improving air quality will directly benefit families, reducing the risk of respiratory diseases.

Indicator 3.4.1: Decrease in mortality from non-communicable diseases.

  • Creating spaces that promote physical activity, such as walking trails and playgrounds, will contribute to healthier lifestyles.
Hub for Learning and Skill Development

Relevant SDG: SDG 4: Quality Education

Indicator 4.3.1: Participation rate in technical and vocational education programs.

  • Building a campus with accessible training programs will provide people opportunities to learn practical skills.
  • The programs will be tailored to align with industry needs, improving employability rates.

Indicator 4.4.1: Increase in youth and adult skills for employment and entrepreneurship.

  • By connecting students with real-world projects, we aim to develop skills like problem-solving, innovation, and teamwork.
Better Industry and Infrastructure

Relevant SDG: SDG 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure

Indicator 9.1.2: Improved access to transportation infrastructure.

  • We aim to build better roads and transportation systems that connect urban and rural areas efficiently.

Indicator 9.4.1: Use of environmentally friendly technologies.

  • Our goal is to implement renewable energy solutions in industries, reducing greenhouse gas emissions.
Sustainable Cities and Communities

Relevant SDG: SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities

Indicator 11.2.1: Access to public transportation systems.

  • We will focus on creating affordable and accessible public transportation to reduce reliance on private vehicles.

Indicator 11.6.2: Management of municipal and other waste.

  • Incorporating better waste management systems will ensure cleaner cities and reduce environmental pollution.
Working Together for Change

Relevant SDG: SDG 17: Partnerships for the Goals

Indicator 17.17.1: Partnerships between public, private, and civil sectors.

  • We will collaborate with various stakeholders to pool resources and expertise for impactful projects.

Indicator 17.6.1: Knowledge sharing and cooperation for access to technology.

  • Sharing research and innovation across sectors will help us achieve sustainable development goals more effectively.
