Goals and SDG Integration

Livable Areas for Families and Community

Relevant SDG: SDG 3: Good Health and Well-Being

  • We want to make neighborhoods where families can live happily with access to parks and open spaces.
  • The plan is to improve safety and create spaces where everyone feels welcome and included.
Hub for Learning and Skill Development

Relevant SDG: SDG 4: Quality Education

  • We aim to build a campus where people can learn new skills and improve their knowledge.
  • It will connect students with industries so they can get hands-on experience.
Better Industry and Infrastructure

Relevant SDG: SDG 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure

  • The goal is to set up strong infrastructure that helps the economy and supports people’s needs.
  • We want to use green technology so the infrastructure is eco-friendly.
Sustainable Cities and Communities

Relevant SDG: SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities

  • The idea is to plan cities that grow in a way that doesn’t harm the environment.
  • We’ll focus on improving public transport and using technology to make cities smarter.
Working Together for Change

Relevant SDG: SDG 17: Partnerships for the Goals

  • We need to build strong partnerships with different organizations to make this happen.
  • Sharing resources and knowledge will help us achieve bigger results faster.
