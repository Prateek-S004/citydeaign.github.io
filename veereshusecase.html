Mapping of buisness case studies with sustainable goals

Target 11.2: Safe and Sustainable Transportation

Indicator 11.2.1: The percentage of people who can easily access public transport, broken down by sex, age, and disabilities.

  • Building public transport systems that are not only efficient but also accessible for everyone.
  • Making sure that public transport is affordable and safe for all groups, including women, children, and the elderly.
Target 11.4: Protect Cultural and Natural Heritage

Indicator 11.4.1: How much money is spent per person on preserving and protecting cultural and natural heritage, both from public and private sources.

  • Making sure we protect historic sites and natural landmarks in urban areas.
  • Promoting cultural tourism in a way that doesn't harm heritage sites.
Target 11.6: Waste Management and Air Quality

Indicator 11.6.1: The percentage of municipal waste that is collected and managed properly, rather than being dumped in uncontrolled sites.

  • Setting up effective waste management systems to reduce landfill overflow and encourage recycling.

Indicator 11.6.2: Average levels of harmful particulate matter (like PM2.5 and PM10) in the air in cities.

  • Improving air quality by reducing pollution from factories and vehicles.
  • Promoting green spaces and eco-friendly transport options in cities.
Target 11.7: Accessible Green and Public Spaces

Indicator 11.7.1: The percentage of urban areas that is open space for public use, accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities.

  • Creating parks and public spaces that are inclusive and accessible for all, no matter their physical abilities.

Indicator 11.7.2: The percentage of people who have been victims of crime in the last year in urban areas.

  • Making cities safer through better lighting, surveillance, and community programs.
Target 11.C: Financial Support for Sustainable Urban Development

Indicator 11.C.1: The proportion of financial support going to least developed countries (LDCs) for building sustainable, resilient, and energy-efficient buildings.

  • Encouraging investment in green infrastructure, especially in developing regions.
  • Promoting technologies that make buildings more energy-efficient and environmentally friendly.
