Renewable Energy

The city will harness solar and wind energy to produce clean power, reducing reliance on fossil fuels.

Green Buildings

Construction will focus on energy-efficient buildings that consume less electricity and water.

Smart Transportation

The city will promote public transport, cycling, and electric vehicles to decrease traffic congestion and air pollution.

Waste Management

Comprehensive recycling programs will be implemented, alongside facilities that convert waste into energy.

Water Conservation

Smart water monitoring systems will be employed to optimize usage and prevent wastage.

Parks and Green Spaces

The development of parks and community gardens will provide natural habitats and recreational areas.

Sustainable Farming

Initiatives for greenhouse and mushroom farming will encourage local food production while minimizing environmental impact.

Research and Development

Support will be provided for labs and projects focused on eco-friendly technologies and sustainable solutions.

Community Involvement

Residents will be encouraged to participate in green initiatives, promoting recycling and sustainable practices.

Protecting Nature

The city will designate protected areas to conserve biodiversity and restore natural habitats for local wildlife.
