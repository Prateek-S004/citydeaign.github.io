Vyomadri Smart City Development

Home Revenue Sustainability Zone Distribution

The development of Vyomadri is divided into two phases. Phase 1 focuses on building core infrastructure, residential areas, and commercial zones. Phase 2 will emphasize expanding other zones, including parks, renewable energy, healthcare, and public spaces.

Phase 1: Core Development Areas

Zone Type Revised Area (Phase 1) Phase 1 Budget Focus/Key Components

Low-Density Residential 70 sq. km ₹12,000 crore Gated communities and eco-friendly housing.
Commercial Zones 35 sq. km ₹15,000 crore Business districts with early-stage corporate offices and retail expansion.
Industrial Zones 60 sq. km ₹20,000 crore Establish core manufacturing and industrial hubs.
Port and Logistics 55 sq. km ₹28,000 crore Focus on cargo, warehousing, and trade facilities.
